Real Estate BI Demo Brief

Problem Statement

Real-estate sales and finance teams often work across disconnected project inventory, enquiry funnels, payment-plan commitments, collections schedules and handover readiness lists. This makes it difficult to understand true booked value, receivable risk, inventory absorption, conversion quality and possession readiness from one executive view.

My Solution

I built a Cloudflare-hosted Real Estate Revenue Intelligence dashboard with Auth0-protected access, dynamic slicers, real-estate KPI cards, project inventory analysis, payment plan analytics, lead funnel tracking, collections and overdue risk, handover readiness, six-month forecast planning and a Groq-backed AI Analyst.
